Donations headline for PCM

Did You Know…
… that Protestant Campus Ministry (PCM) is not funded by Stony Brook University? Support for PCM comes from local churches, some of their governing bodies and community members. Stony Brook University has given us office space and free use of meeting rooms for our campus events, but for everything else—bible study materials, hospitality, equipment, office supplies, transportation, printing, and stipends— we are dependent on you. The Protestant Campus Ministry provides quality spiritual support and programs for adults during critical years. Our on-campus Christian chaplaincy offers a safe environment where students from diverse backgrounds can come together to honestly question, grow and live out their faith. PCM extends a warm welcome to college students, and travels with them through all the twists, turns and detours of their spiritual journeys. Throughout the academic year, the chaplain’s office at Protestant Campus Ministry sponsors prayer groups and leads activities focused on spiritual health, formation and transformation.

Your donation can help PCM spread values that provide a safe environment for students to practice their faith.

Everyone is on their own journey where they might be trying to figure themselves out and grow as individuals. Donating even a small amount can help a lot in providing a safe environment for our students to practice their faith.
