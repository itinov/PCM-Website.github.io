About Protestant Campus Ministries at Stony Brook University

Protestant Campus Ministry (PCM) is an ecumenical Christian organization dedicated to the spiritual and intellectual development of the Stony Brook Community. Under the leadership of our Director & Campus Pastor, Rev. Brenda D. Ford, we welcome dreams of what authentic life together would look like here at Stony Brook University. We want to be an Acts 2 community where students, faculty and staff together can:
Be... themselves, beloved children of God
Belong... to a world transforming community
Become... who God is calling us to become.
