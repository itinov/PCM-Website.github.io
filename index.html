About Protestant Campus Ministries at Stony Brook University

Protestant Campus Ministry (PCM) is an ecumenical Christian organization dedicated to the spiritual and intellectual development of the Stony Brook Community. Under the leadership of our Director & Campus Pastor, Rev. Brenda D. Ford, we welcome dreams of what authentic life together would look like here at Stony Brook University. We want to be an Acts 2 community where students, faculty and staff together can:
Be... themselves, beloved children of God
Belong... to a world transforming community
Become... who God is calling us to become.

Your donation can save
many lives

Did You Know… … that Protestant Campus Ministry (PCM) is not funded by Stony Brook University? Support for PCM comes from local churches, some of their governing bodies and community members. Stony Brook University has given us office space and free use of meeting rooms for our campus events, but for everything else—bible study materials, hospitality, equipment, office supplies, transportation, printing, and stipends— we are dependent on you. The Protestant Campus Ministry provides quality spiritual support and programs for adults during critical years. Our on-campus Christian chaplaincy offers a safe environment where students from diverse backgrounds can come together to honestly question, grow and live out their faith. PCM extends a warm welcome to college students, and travels with them through all the twists, turns and detours of their spiritual journeys. Throughout the academic year, the chaplain’s office at Protestant Campus Ministry sponsors prayer groups and leads activities focused on spiritual health, formation and transformation.
